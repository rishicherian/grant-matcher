2026 Community Empowerment Grants
---------------------------------
Applications are now open for the **2026 Community Empowerment Grants**. This annual program provides funding  to   local community-based organizations to expand their   impact and  strengthen programs that support men and boys across Boston’s neighborhoods.
The Community Empowerment Small Grants are awarded to local community-based organizations to scale up their work.  This year, a total of $500,000 will be awarded to nonprofit organizations throughout Boston's various neighborhoods.
Application
##### Timeline and Information Dates:
* **Application Launch Date**: October 30, 2025
* **Information Session**: Thursday November 13, 2025, (Meeting Link)
* **Application Deadline:**Monday, December 1st, 2025 at 5:00 p.m.
* **Award Notification:**Mid December 2025
* **Grant Period:** Grant-funded activities must be completed byJune 30, 2026
* **Reporting Due Dates:** March 1st, 2026 and August 1st, 2026
