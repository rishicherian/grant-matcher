Request for Proposals – Resilient Philadelphia: Citywide Climate Resilience Plan Update
=======================================================================================
**A virtual RFP information session was held on April 2, 2025 from 3:00-4:00pm. View the info session recording and slide deck.**
**All questions concerning this RFP must be submitted in writing via email to abby.sullivan@phila.gov, with a cc: to emily.gowen@phila.gov by April 9th, 2025 with the subject line “Questions: Citywide Climate Resilience Plan Update”.**
**Proposals must be received no later than 9:00pm Philadelphia, PA, local time, on April 27, 2025. A .pdf version of the proposal must be emailed to: abby.sullivan@phila.gov, with a cc: to emily.gowen@phila.gov with the subject line “Proposal: Citywide Climate Resilience Plan Update”.**
The Philadelphia City Fund and the City of Philadelphia’s Office of Sustainability (“OOS”)  seek qualified professional firms (individual firms or consultant teams) to provide climate resilience planning and engagement services for this project Resilient Philadelphia: Citywide Resilience Plan Update. The selected firm will work with OOS to conduct a Climate Resilience Needs and Values Assessment (“NVA”) and update the City’s Climate Resilience Plan. Additional Expectations are outlined in full in the RFP.
