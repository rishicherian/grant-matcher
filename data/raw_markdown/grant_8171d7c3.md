The Pawtucket Foundation is excited to welcome Kelly Castaños as our new Engagement Manager! A Rhode Island native, Kelly brings a strong background in public service, technology, and community engagement. Her experience spans roles with the RI Department of Education, Neighborhood Health Plan of Rhode Island, and the Executive Office of the Governor. In these roles, Kelly advanced initiatives through data-driven strategy, cross-sector collaboration, and a deep commitment to community impact. Kelly is also the founder of RIGSC, a social and professional network dedicated to fostering authentic connections and creating inclusive spaces for women across Rhode Island. In her new role, Kelly will focus on strengthening relationships with local stakeholders, enhancing member engagement, and supporting initiatives that drive economic development, placement, and connectivity in City of Pawtucket, RI
Please join us in welcoming Kelly to the team!
