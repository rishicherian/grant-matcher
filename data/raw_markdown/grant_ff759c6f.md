Vision Statement
----------------
The City of Dover is a place where people want to live! Through the team effort of its elected officials,
employees, and citizens, Dover is a clean and safe community with a future of balanced growth and
opportunity where all citizens are heard, enjoy a high quality of life, and diversity is valued.
