State awards $1.2M in grants to support fishing industry
========================================================
Kathryn Gallerani
The Standard-Times
Dec. 16, 2025, 4:02 a.m. ET
New Bedford and other ports are working hard to support the fishing industry in Massachusetts, and nearly $1.2 million has been approved to help increase their efforts.
Gov. Maura Healey has awarded the funding to support the fishing industry using innovation and community resilience funding.
The state Division of Marine Fisheries, working alongside sister agencies within the Department of Energy and Environmental Affairs, helps ensure the commercial fishing industry can remain profitable, resilient, and safe during the development of offshore wind in the region, according to a written release about the funding.
