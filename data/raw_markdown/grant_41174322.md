Family-Friendly Activities and Events in Dover, DE
--------------------------------------------------
In Dover, visitors can spend their days doing as much or as little as they want to feed their souls or feel invigorated. Whether you prefer quality me-time fishing in one of Dover’s parks or engaging in exciting community services and events, creating a fun-filled Dover weekend itinerary is easy.
Enjoy a classic seafood restaurant with a fresh catch at Cool Springs Fish Bar, or take advantage of tax-free shopping in Downtown Dover, where you will discover enchanting local merchants such as Forney’s Too.
Dover’s cultural scene is always buzzing with activities. Enjoy dining and drinks at McGlynns Pub or happy hour at Grey Fox Grille. For entertainment and plenty of laughter, try line dancing at 9 East Taphouse, or catch live music, theater productions and festivals throughout the year.
Discover friendly gathering places where even your four-legged family is welcomed. The beer garden at Rail Haus in Downtown Dover is the perfect place to regroup between excursions. Experience the warmth and hospitality that make Dover a setting where lasting memories are made.
