SOUTH JERSEY
Camden parks to see $5.7M for upgrades. Here's what to expect
=============================================================
Kaitlyn McCormick
Cherry Hill Courier-Post
Oct. 6, 2025, 10:49 a.m. ET
Parks and community spaces in the City of Camden are receiving more than $5 million for upgrades funded by the Urban Investment Fund Grant Program of the New Jersey Economic Development Authority.
New safety features, landscaping, public art, playground equipment and more are set for spots throughout the city as a means to attract students, residents and visitors to the Camden's communal spaces.
