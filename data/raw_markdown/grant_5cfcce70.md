Find grants and funding opportunities with our updated funding database!
========================================================================
By Charlotte
Steinecke Posted: Jan 10, 2025
Funding for programs can come from a wide variety of sources – federal, state, and local governments, foundations large and small, and other donor groups. Tracking down those funding streams and determining eligibility takes a lot of time and resources, which we know are in short supply.
That’s why we are so excited to reveal our updated Funding Database! Newly refreshed for 2025, the database has the latest information and leads on funding applications to fit your program.
The Funding Database allows you to search for funding by program area, program age, state, and agency or office, as well as filter by funding type (private or government). Application deadlines, eligibility requirements, and a brief description of the opportunities are available at a glance, and one click will get you to the details of the funding announcement.
Check out the Funding Database and use it for your sustainability strategy in 2025. We are always looking to update the list, so if you have a lead to share, please send it to us at info@afterschoolalliance.org.
