Overseen by the Catholic Community Fund, the Catholic Health Foundation (CHF) exists to provide financial support to non-profit organizations within the 144 cities and towns of the Archdiocese of Boston that provide health care and healthcare services consistent with the teachings of the Roman Catholic Church.
Since it was formed as a result of the sale of Caritas hospitals in 2013, CHF has awarded 841 grants totaling over $8 million.

Grant Making
------------
The FY26 Request for Proposals is now open. The deadline for applications is **Friday, April 10, 2026**.
Grants are annually distributed in the following categories:
* Bereavement
* Building/Equipment
* Cancer
* Education (medical or health)
* Employee assistance
* Health or medical services for residents of Middleborough
* Medical
* Social needs
* Unrestricted
If you have any health-related initiatives in your parish or school, social service agency, or other nonprofit entity you may be involved in, we invite you to apply.
Key Dates
* **Friday, February 6, 2026:** Request for Proposals (RFP) opens
* **Friday, April 10, 2026:** Deadline for applications
* **June 2026:** Decisions made and approved
* **July 2026:** Grants distributed
