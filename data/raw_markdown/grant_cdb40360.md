Evelyn W. Preston Memorial Fund – Connecticut
=============================================
Dated: February 11, 2025
The Evelyn W. Preston Memorial Fund provides grants for music concerts during the months of June, July, August, and September in parks and other public places for the general public in the City of Hartford.
**Donor Name:** Evelyn W. Preston Memorial Fund
**State:**Connecticut
**City:**Hartford
**Type of Grant:** Grant
**Deadline:** 02/28/2025
**Size of the Grant:** $100,000 to $500,000
**Grant Duration:** 2 Years
**Details:**
**Program Types**
Arts, Culture, & Humanities.
**Funding Information**
Foundation have budgeted $200,000 per year between April 2025 – December 2027 for this project. Applicants should not request more than $200,000 for each year.
**Grant Period**
April 2025 – December 2027.
**Eligibility Criteria**
Applications will be accepted from nonprofit organizations such as arts organizations, social service agencies, senior centers, faith-based organizations, folk societies and cultural centers and colleges/universities. The Fund program will consider applications from individual artists working in partnership with a nonprofit organization or church serving as their fiscal sponsor.
Applicants must meet the following criteria to apply:
* Organizations must have an IRS Tax Determination letter 501(c)(3)
* Concerts to be held for the general public in the City of Hartford parks, or other appropriate open spaces or public locations
* 2 or more instruments must be played to be considered a music concert.
For more information, visit Bank of America.
