Augusta Health in Virginia announced that Shenandoah Women’s Healthcare will join the medical group Aug. 1.
“The addition of these experienced doctors, advanced practice practitioners, midwives, nurses and their teams to Augusta Health’s medical group will expand access to OB-GYN care across the Shenandoah Valley,” said Mary Mannix, President and Chief Executive Officer at Augusta Health.
The partnership will give patients access to the resources of Augusta Health, including support from the Mayo Clinical Care Network, care from Augusta Health’s nursing team, and access to the new Augusta Health’s Outpatient Pavilion.
Augusta Health is an independent, community-owned, not-for-profit hospital. Read more.
