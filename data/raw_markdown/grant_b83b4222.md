WESTERN CONNECTICUT AREA AGENCY ON AGING: Area Agencies on Aging - Waterbury
============================================================================
Share:
Area agency on aging plans, advocates, funds, and coordinates programs for elderly, caregivers, and younger disabled individuals in the Western Conn. region. Also offers the CHOICES Program that provides older adults with health insurance counseling, information and referral to senior services, and eligibility screening for general State and Federal benefits programs. Also offers the Respite Care Program that provides primary caregivers of persons with Alzheimer's/Parkinson or dementia related disorders with limited funding for home care/respite services to relieve caregiver stress; National Family Caregivers Program that provides primary caregivers of persons over the age of 60 with limited funding for respite care or supplemental services.
