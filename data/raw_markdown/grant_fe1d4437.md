Description
-----------
The Baird Foundation primarily supports nonprofit organizations in the western New York area, with emphasis on Buffalo.
The Foundation's areas of interest include:
* animal welfare;
* arts and culture;
* children and youth;
* civic affairs;
* education;
* the environment;
* health;
* historical preservation;
* museums; and,
* social services.
The Foundation provides support for special projects and capital needs.
