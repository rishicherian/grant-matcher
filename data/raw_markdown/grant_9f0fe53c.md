Mini-Grant
----------
It takes vision, resilience, and deep commitment to launch a nonprofit organization, and NewYork-Presbyterian is here to support that journey. Mini-Grants are designed to uplift small, grassroots nonprofits in New York City and Westchester County that are making a difference through innovative, community-driven health and wellness programs.
“Our experience as an NYP mini-grantee has been AMAZING! We so appreciate your support and passion to serve our community! It shows in every single thing you do!”
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mini-Grants is a three-year grant helps build capacity for organizations with limited resources by combining funding with educational and skill-building opportunities.
**Funding includes:**
* Year 1: $7,500
* Year 2: Up to $10,000
* Year 3: Up to $12,500
**To be considered for funding, applicants must:**
* Be a registered 501(c)(3), government agency, or operate under a fiscal sponsor
* Have an annual operating budget under $500,000
* Focus on health and wellness in underserved communities
