**Project Title:** Fox-Hollow Redevelopment
**Description of the Proposed Project:** Fox Hollow is an existing and operational 22-unit residential development located at 15- 36 Desmund Run, Sicklerville, Winslow Township, Camden County, NJ 08081. There are 22 single story attached bungalows grouped in two (2) clusters on the 12.927-acre site. Although composed of 22 lots, the site is a contiguous assemblage. Passive landscaping, off-street parking and a quiet public right-of-way compose the site features. The buildings are connected by a network of concrete sidewalks.
