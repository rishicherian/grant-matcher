### **Mayor Kenneth J. Hopkins**
**Welcome to the City of Cranston! Our city is proudly ranked the #1 Best Place to Live in Rhode Island for 2025-2026 by Real Estate U.S. News & World Report,****among Livability.com's 'Top Best Places to Live in The U.S. in 2023; and** **America's 50 Best Cities to Live as selected by Fortune Magazine in 2023!**
**As the second largest City in the State of Rhode Island, Cranston is home to over**82,000 residents.********Cranston proudly boasts t**wo of our State's premier shopping and dining centers - Garden City Center and Chapel View. Being a vibrant suburban community, Cranston also vaunts rural areas on the western side of the City and a shoreline on beautiful Narragansett Bay to the east.**
