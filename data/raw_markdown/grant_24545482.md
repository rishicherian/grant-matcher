Child and Dependent Care Credit
-------------------------------
For many of us juggling work and parenthood, the Child and Dependent Care Credit offers essential relief by helping cover the costs of child care.
This tax credit is specifically designed to aid working parents, providing a percentage of childcare expenses, up to $3,000 for one child or $6,000 for two or more children. The exact percentage of expenses covered ranges from 20% to 35%, depending on our income level.
To qualify for this credit, we must meet several criteria:
1. **Earned Income**: We need to have income from employment or self-employment.
2. **Work-Related Care**: The child care services must be used to allow us to work or actively look for work.
3. **Child Care Regulated**: The care provider must meet state regulations, ensuring our children are in safe and qualified hands.
