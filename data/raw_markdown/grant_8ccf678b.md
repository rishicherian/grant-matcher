* Syracuse, New York Veteran Grants
Are you a veteran residing in Syracuse, New York? The United States Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) may have grants and funding programs for veterans in Syracuse, New York. Typically, in order to qualify, you must be:
* An active member of the Army, Navi, Air Force, Marine Corps, or Coast Guard residing in Syracuse, New York.
* Not an active member and discharged from the Army, Navi, Air Force, Marine Corps, or Coast Guard residing in Syracuse, New York.
* Commissioned officers in eligible government sectors residing in Syracuse, New York.
In addition, there may be business grants for veterans residing in Syracuse, New York, who operate a business in Syracuse, New York.
To apply for veteran grants in Syracuse, New York, contact New York financial aid agencies, or
