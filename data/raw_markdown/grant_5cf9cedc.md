Welcome
-------
To ensure that all people with disabilities are afforded the opportunities to exercise all the rights and responsibilities accorded to citizens of this state and each person with a disability is able to reach his/her maximum potential in independence, human development, productivity and self-sufficiency.
**To download the GCD Assistance Form please follow the steps below:**
1. Download GCD Assistance Form
2. Save to your Computer
3. Fill Out GCD Assistance Form
4. Save a Copy for your records
5. Email the GCD Assistance form to GCD.Disabilities@gcd.ri.gov
6. Please Note the Highlighted "Submit form" is not a Hyperlink. It is a reminder to send as a "PDF" Document Attachment
7. The "Example date" on the form can be over-written. Please input your current date on the form.
