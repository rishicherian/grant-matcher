AI FOR
SOCIAL
GOOD
====================
The AI For Social Good (AI4SG) project offers  curriculum that engages students from all disciplines to propose or develop AI powered solutions that address pressing issues in their communities aligning with United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs). This project is sponsored by National Science Foundation.
