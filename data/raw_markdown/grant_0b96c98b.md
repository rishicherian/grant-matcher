**Burlington County Local Arts Grant 2026**
===========================================
**Supporting The Arts in Burlington County**
============================================
The Local Arts Program (LAP) provides funding to local nonprofit organizations (501 (c) (3), 501 (c) (4), and 501 (c) (6)) from the New Jersey State Council on the Arts (NJSCA)/Department of State, a Partner Agency of the National Endowment for the Arts. General Operating Support and Special Arts Project grants are available to qualified organizations.
