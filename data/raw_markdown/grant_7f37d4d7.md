Request Funding
---------------
Do you have a community project you think might fit our guidelines - primarily, brick-and-mortar with a 20-year lifespan? We would be happy to talk about your ideas even before you complete your application.​
Reach out for more information or to request funding, at info@montpelierfoundation.org. Funding requests must include the following information:
* Name of Applicant and Organization Name (if applicable)
* Phone Number
* Email Address
* Mailing Address
* Website (if applicable)
* Brief description of the organization (if applicable).
* Have you / your organization received support from the Montpelier Foundation in the past?
* Description of the proposed project for which you are seeking funds.
* Total amount needed to fund this project and total amount you are requesting from the Montpelier Foundation (Please note - most of our grants are between $1,000-$5,000, and we rarely provide more than $10,000 toward a project).
* Please include the total amount to be raised and a list of expected sources of funds.
* How does your proposed project provide long-term public benefits in Montpelier? Estimate of the number of people who would benefit from this project on an annual basis
* If your request is approved, how will the Montpelier Foundation be recognized?
