Geographic Focus
----------------
City of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania only. Organizations must be located in Philadelphia or focused on local Philadelphia issues.
Funding Priorities
------------------
* **Movement building** - organizations engaged in two or more movement-building strategies
* **Advocating for policies** that lead to equitable treatment, economic stability, and opportunity
* **Confronting and exposing harmful institutions and systems** and reimagining a world where policies are designed with and by historically excluded communities
* **Promoting collective healing and joy** within and among historically excluded communities
* **Power-building activities** for marginalized communities
* **Social, racial, and economic justice** in Philadelphia
What They Don't Fund
--------------------
* Activities or services taking place outside of Philadelphia (unless significant impact on Philadelphia)
* Organizations not involved in social justice, movement building, and/or systems change work
* Leadership Development and Wellness grants (automatically awarded to grantee partners, no separate application)
Application Method
------------------
Two-stage process:
1. **Stage 1**: Funding inquiry submission (reviewed within 4-6 weeks)
2. **Stage 2**: Full proposal by invitation only (includes conversation with staff, written questions, organizational demographics, financial statements)
**Current Status**: Grant cycle is closed until 2027. Three-year general operating grants awarded to cohorts.
**Eligibility**: Organizations with 501(c)(3) status or using fiscal sponsor, focused on Philadelphia.
