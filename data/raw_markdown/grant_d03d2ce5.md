Apply for Funds & Grants
========================
The Executive Office of Housing (EOH) provides funding opportunities to support programs that expand housing production and preservation, improve community development, and address homelessness across Rhode Island. Through competitive grants and funding initiatives, EOH partners with municipalities, nonprofit organizations, and service providers to invest in solutions that create safe, stable, and affordable housing for all Rhode Islanders. On this page, you can explore available funding opportunities, eligibility requirements, and application guidance to find the right funding for your project.
