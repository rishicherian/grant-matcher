Grants
======
The Foundation provides grants to non-profits, public charities and tax-exempt organizations whose missions include assisting those in need.  One of the Foundation's core values is enriching the lives of individuals who are or have struggled and who are working toward a brighter future.  The Foundation is specifically interested in the following areas of giving:
education, youth, elderly & disabled, low income, housing & homelessness, and programs which foster social responsibility.
Grants are reserved for tax-exempt organizations in Rockingham County (NH), Hillsborough County (NH), Essex County (MA), and Middlesex County (MA) or non-profits with specific programs that serve individuals in this region.
