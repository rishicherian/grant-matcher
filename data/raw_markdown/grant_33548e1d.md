Grants & Awards
===============
Farm Credit East offers financial grants and recognition to those who support agriculture and vitalize our rural communities.
AgEnhancement Grants
--------------------
Ranging from $500 to $10,000, AgEnhancement Grants were established to support the wide range of projects necessary to build and promote stronger agriculture, forest products, commercial fishing and related industries.
AgEnhancement Grants
Farmers Market Grants
---------------------
Farmers markets provide an important channel for Northeast producers to market and sell their products as well as connect with consumers. That's why we've seen a significant increase in these markets across the region over the past two decades. To support this important marketing channel, Farm Credit East offers Farmers Market Grants up to $500.
Farmers Market Grants
Food Access Infrastructure Initiative Grants
--------------------------------------------
Farm Credit East's Food Access Infrastructure Initiative financially supports organizations with grants of up to $50,000 as they work to improve food security in the Northeast. If your organization, or one you know, has an upcoming infrastructure project to further food access, apply for a Food Access Infrastructure Initiative grant. Organizations must be located within Farm Credit East's territory (Connecticut, Maine, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, Rhode Island and Vermont). Awards will be made at the sole discretion of the selection committee.
Food Access Infrastructure Initiative Grant
Lipinski Rural Initiatives Award
--------------------------------
Former Farm Credit East CEO Bill Lipinski was an advocate for Northeast rural communities. That is why the Farm Credit East Board of Directors established the Lipinski Rural Initiatives Award upon his retirement – to grow the organization's support of rural communities in his honor.
Lipinski Award
Grants & Incentives for Northeast Agriculture
---------------------------------------------
This document contains grants and other incentives available in the Northeast states of Connecticut, Maine, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, Rhode Island and Vermont, serviced by Farm Credit East. The listings include grant programs, tax incentives and loan programs from various funding sources available to agricultural producers and other entities involved in agriculture or related industries.
