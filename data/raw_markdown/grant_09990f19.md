Funding Opportunities
=====================
Funds administered by the Community Development Division (CDD), with a few exceptions, are made available to eligible community partners through a Request for Proposals (RFP) process. The RFPs include information about the application process, criteria for successful applications and the amount of funding available. To receive emails about funding opportunities please subscribe to the CDD Funding Opportunities email list.
