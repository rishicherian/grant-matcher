#### **About Organisation**
John and Maria Laffin Trust is a charitable foundation that provides funding to nonprofit organizations working in education, animal welfare, medical research, and human services. The Trust is committed to supporting initiatives that improve academic quality, promote humane animal care, advance innovative medical research, and alleviate human suffering. With a focus on meaningful and measurable impact, it distributes approximately 25 grants annually. By prioritizing both local and global initiatives, the Trust plays a vital role in supporting nonprofits that address critical social, scientific, and humanitarian challenges.
#### **About the Grant**
The John and Maria Laffin Trust Grants 2026 program provides financial support to nonprofit organizations working across four key sectors: education, animal welfare, medical research, and human services. This nonprofit grant USA supports projects that enhance academic excellence, improve animal care, advance innovative medical solutions, and address poverty and human suffering. The program funds both local initiatives in Los Angeles and global projects in research and humanitarian work. It is ideal for organizations seeking small grants for NGOs to implement impactful, focused projects with measurable outcomes in social and scientific development.
#### **Who can Apply?**
Eligible applicants must be nonprofit organizations recognized as tax-exempt under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Organizations must align with one of the Trust’s four focus areas: education, animal welfare, medical research, or human services. Education and animal welfare applicants must operate within Los Angeles City or County, while medical research and human services projects are open globally. Applicants should demonstrate strong organizational capacity, clear objectives, and measurable outcomes. Proposals must highlight the project’s relevance and impact within the selected focus area, ensuring alignment with the Trust’s mission.
#### **How to Apply?**
Confirm your organization’s eligibility as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit and ensure alignment with one of the Trust’s focus areas. Prepare application materials including a detailed project description, objectives, and budget. Highlight measurable impact and relevance to the selected category. Submit your application following the Trust’s official guidelines. Applications are reviewed based on alignment, feasibility, and potential impact before funding decisions are made.
#### **Application Deadline**
The application deadline for the John and Maria Laffin Trust Grants 2026 is 31 July 2026. Applicants should prepare early to ensure complete and well-structured submissions.
