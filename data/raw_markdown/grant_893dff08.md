DEC announced the fifth round of competitive grants for the Forest Conservation Easements for Land Trusts (FCELT) Grant Program. In partnership with the Land Trust Alliance, a total of $1.35 million in grant funding is available for eligible, accredited land trusts to purchase conservation easements on privately owned forested land to protect these lands from future development.
Projects supported by the FCELT Grant Program will help conserve the benefits of forests, including:
👍 Supporting climate resiliency
👍 Reducing erosion
👍 Enhancing flood control
👍 Providing wildlife habitat
👍 Filtering water and air
👍 Mitigating climate change
The goal of the grant program is to increase the pace of forest land conservation to keep forests as forests, mitigate the impacts of climate change, and help reach New York State’s goal of conserving 30% of the state’s lands and waters by 2030 Applications are due by 5:00 p.m. on August 13, 2026.
For more info on the FCELT grant, visit: https://lnkd.in/e9kquuv2
