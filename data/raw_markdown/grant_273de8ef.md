Description:
This competitive solicitation seeks applicants to administer Community Development Block Grant (CDBG) Social Services and Emergency Solutions Grant (ESG) programs for the Camden County Office of Community Development. The award will fund a range of social service initiatives—such as emergency shelter, homelessness prevention, and family assistance—to improve quality of life for residents in need.
• Eligible entities may include nonprofit service providers, community‑based organizations, or local agencies that can deliver direct social services.
• Funding will be allocated to projects that align with CDBG and ESG priorities, including rapid rehousing, supportive services, and emergency crisis response.
• Applicants must submit a detailed work plan, budget, and compliance documentation; the County will evaluate proposals based on impact, capacity, and cost‑effectiveness.
