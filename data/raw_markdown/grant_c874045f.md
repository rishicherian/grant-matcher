Description
-----------
The Anne M. and Philip H. Glatfelter, III Family Foundation supports nonprofit organizations in southeastern Pennsylvania, with emphasis on the Harrisburg area.
The Foundation's areas of interest include:
* arts and culture;
* education;
* the environment;
* healthcare;
* religion; and,
* social welfare.
The Foundation provides the following types of grants:
* one-time grants, which are single year grants for a specific purpose;
