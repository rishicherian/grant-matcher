**Cape Fear Memorial Bridge**
The Cape Fear Memorial Bridge opened October 1, 1969.  It is a steel vertical-lift bridge that raises and lowers to allow river traffic through.  It constitutes US 17/US 76/US 421 across the Cape Fear River between Brunswick County and New Hanover County, North Carolina.  See the river face of the city.
