Tapestry Childhood Education Platform & App
===========================================
Nurturing Children, Educators and Families
------------------------------------------
Tapestry has the tools you need to help you run your setting or school smoothly and to celebrate, support and engage with children, families and staff. Created by educators for educators, **we’re the original and leading Childhood Education Platform** celebrating children and engaging families through EYFS and Key Stage 1 and 2, in mainstream and SEND provision.
What is Tapestry?
-----------------
Available both as a website and app, Tapestry is the Childhood Education Platform that provides comprehensive, award-winning tools to help you build strong relationships with families, and reduce barriers to engagement. You can document each child’s unique learning and development and streamline your planning, monitoring and reporting processes through EYFS to KS1 and 2, for mainstream and special provision.
Nurseries and childminders can also benefit from Tapestry’s **Nursery Management Software**, which provides clear, transparent and accurate invoicing, smart occupancy planning and in-app attendance registers for children and staff.

Why choose Tapestry?
--------------------
Because these four principles form the **heart** of our Childhood Education Platform
### Nurturing Relationships with Families
Work closely with parents and carers to build and maintain communication, collaboration and engagement, creating a supportive environment that celebrates every child’s growth and learning.
**Support Parent Partnerships.**
### Supporting your Staff to Elevate your Provision
Reflect on practice and pedagogy individually and as a team to implement inclusive provision that has an impact on learning and development.
**Support Educator Professional Development & Wellbeing.**
### Enabling Child-Centred Assessment and Planning
Put each child at the centre of their development and education, encouraging their interests and following bespoke learning pathways to ensure every child can thrive.
**Support Child-centred Development.**
### Supporting Nursery Management Processes
Use effective management tools such as smart booking, accurate invoicing, in-app attendance registers and adult ratios, to simplify and streamline processes for staff and families.
**Support Nursery Management Processes.**
