sarayut Thaneerat
Pennsylvania is unparalleled in its natural beauty. The efforts to protect that beauty must be equally unparalleled. I am proud to celebrate the decisions that Pennsylvania farmers make as responsible stewards of their land, now and for future generations.
I always appreciate hearing farmers’ stories and learning about their journeys, new practices they’ve implemented, and the significant steps they’ve taken to make investments in conservation.
