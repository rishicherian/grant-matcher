### The 2026 grant application period is from 8:00 am on April 22 to 4:00 pm on June 22, 2026
Growing Greener Plus Grants Program includes:
* Growing Greener Watershed Restoration and Protection
* Surface Mining Conservation and Reclamation Act (SMCRA) Bond Forfeiture
* Stormwater Management Planning (Act 167) Grants
How to Apply
------------
**1. Sign Up:**
Sign up for notifications about the Growing Greener Watershed Protection, EPA 319, and Surface Mining Conservation and Reclamation Act Grant Programs.
**2. Review Materials:**
* 2026 Growing Greener Plus Grants Program Guidance
* Guide to Permitting for Watershed Improvement Projects​
* Application Checklist
* Primer for Fitting Charges within Budget Categories
**3. Watch Video Tutorial**
* Grant Applicant Presentation and Demos
**4. Look Over Examples:**
* AMDTreat Sample Report
* Sample Workplan
* Sample Landowner Letter of Commitment
* Sample Landowner Letter of Commitment (SMCRA)
* Sample Maps and Photos
* Sample Task and Deliverable Budget Worksheets
**5. Complete Forms:**
* Landowner Letters
+ Landowner Letter of Commitment
+ Landowner Letter of Commitment (SMCRA)
* Project Workplan
* Task and Deliverable Budget Worksheets
* Worker Protection and Investment Certification Form
**6. Apply:**
Apply Online and check the eGrants Instructions for Applicants for more details.
**7. Contact Us:**
* Growing Greener Contact Information
* Email at ra-epgrowinggreener@pa.gov
