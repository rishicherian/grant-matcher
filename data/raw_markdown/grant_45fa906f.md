The City of Bridgeport is developing a **Citywide Climate Resiliency Plan**, made possible through funding from the **CT DEEP Climate Resilience Fund**. This plan will help the city prepare for climate challenges, strengthen neighborhoods, protect the environment, and support the local economy.
The City, through its Office of Planning and Economic Development (OPED), is seeking a qualified consultant team to lead the development of the plan. The consultant team will provide services including:
+ Data analysis and research
+ Community engagement
+ Plan preparation, compilation, and adoption
The plan will focus on all neighborhoods in Bridgeport, ensuring that each community is represented. It will include actionable strategies across four main areas:
+ Infrastructure Resilience
+ Social Resilience
+ Environmental Resilience
+ Economic Resilience
The Request for Proposals is now open, and submissions will be accepted until 2:00 PM on November 5th.
Visit https://procurement.opengov.com/portal/bridgeportct for more information.
Bridgeport is committed to building a city that is **resilient, sustainable, and prepared for the future**.
