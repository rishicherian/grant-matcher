------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This waiting list is for Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher rental housing assistance in Augusta, ME.

For more information, visit the Maine State Housing Authority website.

#### How large is the Maine State Housing Authority?
Maine State Housing Authority serves 3,577 low-income
households. The agency manages 4,528 Housing Choice Vouchers and
0 public housing units.
#### What are the incomes of residents served by the Maine State Housing Authority?
Extremely low-income (ELI) renters, earning less than 30% of Area Median Income (AMI), make up
77 percent of households served by Maine State Housing Authority. Very
low-income renters earn less than 50% AMI, and are 95 percent of
Maine State Housing Authority households.
#### How many Maine State Housing Authority residents are employed?
Working adults head 536 households and make up
15 percent of households served by
Maine State Housing Authority.
#### How many female-headed renter households are served by the Maine State Housing Authority?
There are 2539 female-headed renter
households living in Maine State Housing Authority housing. Households headed by females are
71% of those served by the
Maine State Housing Authority.
