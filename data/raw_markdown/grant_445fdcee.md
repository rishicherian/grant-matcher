Before we entirely leave 2025 behind, I have to share one—slightly more personal—highlight about the people we get to know in our community, a small businesses I love, and the local food systems that we fuel our bodies with everyday.
Several years ago a PSE colleague shared that her friend lived in Whatcom County and made the best skyr.
“Skier?” I replied.
“Skyr. Like a yogurt.”
“Ohhh, skyr. Like Greek yogurt.”
Sure, but Icelandic and WAY better.
So I looked for it during my next trip to the farmers market, and had the chance to meet Katherine Steensma. After a sample and an enthusiastic welcome, I was hooked on their delicious locally produced skyr.
Then came a confession: I’ve actually been a dairy connoisseur since a young age when yogurt was the “dessert” my parents allowed. In fact, I even appeared in a Washington Dairy commercial at the age of 4.
So, what was there left to do but visit the Steensma farm and recreate the commercial? Thank you Kate and Ellie for a true highlight of 2025! Go out, get your skyr. Support your local farmers and small businesses.
